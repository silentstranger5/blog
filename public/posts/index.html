How to Make Your Blog

Website constructors

Do you want to create your blog? There are many possible ways to do that. The most popular and accessible ways are targeted for broad and general audiences. Here are some useful links:

  • WordPress is probably the oldest and the most popular website constructor. It has wide customization options.
  • Wix is a fresh solution targeted at a niche audience.
  • Medium is another popular option. I’ve seen many blogs on this platform that discuss more business or technical topics.
